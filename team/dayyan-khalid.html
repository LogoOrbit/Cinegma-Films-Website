Cinegma Films — Producer

Dayyan
Khalid

Film Producer · Assistant Director

Dayyan Khalid is a film producer and assistant director based in Karachi, with a Bachelor's degree in Media Sciences from Indus University. He has produced Zawaal and Portrait of Life, and served as Associate Producer on A Soldier Beside Me, Janjaal, and Peyary Abbu, along with Assistant Producer credit on A Mid Star a Scorching Sun.

Alongside his work on short films, he has also contributed to TV dramas, handling planning, scheduling, on-set coordination, and post-production.
