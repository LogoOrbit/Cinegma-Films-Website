Cinegma Films — DP

Ghayas Uddin
Siddiqui

Director of Photography · Cinematographer

Ghayas Uddin is an internationally focused Director of Photography and Cinematographer specializing in television drama, narrative films, and commercial advertising. With professional experience across broadcast television and branded content, he delivers cinematic visuals that balance storytelling, performance, and commercial impact.

He has worked as Director of Photography on nationally broadcast television dramas, where his work is recognized for cinematic framing, naturalistic lighting, and polished visual execution. In parallel, Ghayas has shot commercial advertisements, brand films, and promotional content for agencies and production teams seeking a high-end, filmic aesthetic.

Ghayas brings a director’s approach to cinematography, understanding narrative structure, emotional beats, and visual continuity. His workflow combines strong creative instincts with deep technical expertise in modern cinema cameras, professional cine lenses, and advanced color grading pipelines—allowing him to adapt seamlessly between long-form drama and fast-paced commercial productions.

Influenced by Asian, Middle Eastern, and international cinema, his visual style avoids conventional television clichés and focuses instead on realism, motivated lighting, and purposeful camera movement. His work is designed to translate across cultures, platforms, and global audiences.

Based in Pakistan and available for international productions, Ghayas Uddin collaborates with directors, producers, agencies, and brands worldwide—offering cinematography services for TV series, feature films, commercials, digital campaigns, and branded storytelling.
