Cinegma Films

Ameer
Hamza

Filmmaker · Writer · Producer

Ameer Hamza is a filmmaker, writer, and producer with a strong passion for storytelling and creating films that carry emotion, meaning, and soul. He has worked on multiple short films, including Janjaal, A Soldier Beside Me, and Zawaal. His film Janjaal has received recognition at different film festivals, including awards and international selections. Ameer believes cinema is not just about visuals, but about making the audience feel something long after the film ends.

His work often explores human emotions, inner conflict, and the realities people silently carry within themselves. He has travelled across Pakistan, interacted with different people, and witnessed stories that inspired him to bring real human experiences to the big picture. As a young filmmaker, he continues to learn, grow, and experiment with different forms of storytelling. For him, filmmaking is not only a profession, but a way to express truth, empathy, and imagination.
