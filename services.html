Professional Film Production & Post-Production Services — Karachi, Pakistan

Cinegma Films offers professional filmmaking services across cinematography, film direction, film production, post-production, and screenwriting, delivered by Syed Asad Raza Abidi — an internationally awarded Pakistani filmmaker based in Karachi. The company has received recognition at 22 international film festivals across 9 countries, including Best Short Film at the Toronto Fantasy/Sci-Fi Film Festival 2026 and Best Director at Cedar Film Festival 2026.

Whether you are a producer seeking a director and editor for a short film or commercial project, a brand looking for cinematic production in Karachi, or an organisation commissioning documentary content, Cinegma Films brings precision, creative vision, and full post-production capability to every engagement. All post-production work is completed using DaVinci Resolve with a professional colour-managed pipeline, and all deliverables are available in broadcast, festival, and web formats.

To discuss your project, contact info@cinegmafilms.com or use the WhatsApp buttons above. Cinegma Films is registered as Cinegma Films LLC and operates from Karachi, Pakistan. Typical response time is within 24–48 hours.
