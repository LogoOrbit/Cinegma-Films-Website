Hire a Pakistani Filmmaker — Karachi-Based Film Production & Post-Production

Cinegma Films accepts commissions and collaborations across film direction, cinematography, film editing, color grading, sound design, and screenwriting. Founded by Syed Asad Raza Abidi — an internationally awarded Pakistani filmmaker based in Karachi — the company has delivered work recognised at 22 international film festivals across 9 countries, including Best Short Film at the Toronto Fantasy/Sci-Fi Film Festival 2026 and Best Director at Cedar Film Festival 2026.

Whether you are a producer seeking a director and editor for a short film or commercial project, a brand looking for cinematic production in Karachi, or an organisation commissioning documentary work, Cinegma Films brings precision, creative vision, and full post-production capability to every project. Email info@cinegmafilms.com with an outline of your project and timeline.

Cinegma Films is registered as Cinegma Films LLC and operates out of Karachi, Pakistan. Response time is typically within 48 hours. For urgent production inquiries or festival programming requests, please note that in the subject line.
