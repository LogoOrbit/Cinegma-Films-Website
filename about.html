The Company

Cinegma
Films LLC

Cinema as the medium. Enigma as the mystery within every frame.

Cinegma Films is an independent film production company founded in January 2024 and based in Karachi, Pakistan. Built around a single conviction — that Pakistani stories deserve to be told with precision, ambition, and without compromise — Cinegma Films operates as a creative home for narrative cinema that challenges, moves, and endures.

The name itself is a declaration of intent: Cinema as the medium, Enigma as the irreducible mystery at the heart of every frame. That philosophy shapes every production — from story selection to final color grade.

In its first year of operation, Cinegma Films produced work that screened across 9 countries, earned international recognition at festivals from Canada to India, Scotland to Kosovo, and achieved a theatrical release at Arena Cinemas, Karachi — an exceptional milestone for an independent student-led production company in Pakistan.

Cinegma Films operates across narrative short films, documentary, and is currently developing its first feature-length projects. The company also serves as a production arm for Silverlight Studios under Burrak Organization.

The Founder

Syed Asad Raza Abidi · Karachi, Pakistan

Syed Asad
Raza Abidi

Filmmaker · Director · Editor · Colorist · Founder

Born on December 9, 2001 in Karachi, Pakistan, Syed Asad Raza Abidi is an independent filmmaker, director, editor, colorist, and the founder of Cinegma Films LLC. He holds a Bachelor of Science in Film & TV Production from Indus University, Karachi (2025), where his passion for storytelling transformed into a precision craft refined through years of hands-on production.

He founded Cinegma Films in early 2024 — an independent production banner built on an uncompromising creative vision. His debut short film, Janjaal (Tussle), became one of the most decorated Pakistani short films in recent memory: shot in just 34 hours across Karachi, then directed, edited, color graded, VFX supervised, and mixed entirely in-house for a 5.1 surround theatrical presentation. Janjaal premiered at Arena Cinemas, Karachi on January 5, 2025 before travelling to film festivals across 9 countries.

Beyond Janjaal, Syed Asad has directed, edited, and color-graded multiple short films including Portrait of Life (2025, his thesis film), the upcoming psychological thriller Zawaal (2026), and Main Aur Achu (2024) — a Silverlight Studios production. He also directed and edited the documentary Urdu Bazaar Karachi, an intimate look inside the city's historic literary market.

In addition to Cinegma Films, he serves as Film Director and Editor at Burrak Organization's Silverlight Studios, where he leads production of narrative Islamic short content. He has also served as Director of Photography and Editor on Phir Se (7T7 Productions) and as Associate Director on Payam e Dil.

BS in Film & TV Production · Indus University, Karachi (2025)  ·  IMDb: nm16938510  ·  FilmFreeway

My vision was much bigger than what we could afford. But that limitation forced us to be more precise, more intentional with every frame. We had 34 hours to make something people would sit still for in a cinema — and they did.

— Syed Asad Raza Abidi, Director, Janjaal
